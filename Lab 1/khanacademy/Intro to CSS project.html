Travel to Los Alamos

It is a county in the U.S. state of New Mexico

Why?

  • There is Los Alamos National Laboratory
  • There was developed Manhattan Project
  • Los Alamos County topped the list as the best place to live in the United States in terms of quality of life in 2004.

High-peak

The county's highest point is located along its northern border, near the summit of Caballo Mountain, at 10,480 feet (3,190 m) above sea level.

Education

All of the county is in the boundary of Los Alamos Public Schools. Los Alamos High School is the comprehensive high school.
